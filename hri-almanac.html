Almanac

Almanac is a funding-prospect tool built for Human Rights Initiative of North Texas (hrionline.org), matching HRI's immigration and human-rights legal-aid mission against public IRS grant-filing data.

This engagement (Almanac + Scribe Pro) is provided to Human Rights Initiative of North Texas as a 90-day in-kind donation.
Every funder listed here traces to a public source (HRI's own site, IRS Form 990 data via ProPublica, or a real grant record in the national Schedule I filing data). Nothing here is estimated or invented. This is a first pipeline pass, not yet a multi-round hand-verified search like Almanac's more mature client builds -- see the methodology note below for exactly what's confirmed and what isn't.

Method

How this was built, and what it cannot tell you

Where this comes from. Every funder and every grant on this page is drawn from that funder's own public record: its IRS Form 990 filings, its published guidelines, and its own grant pages. Each match carries a link back to the source it came from, so nothing here has to be taken on trust. Where a funder does not publish a figure — an award range, a deadline, an eligibility rule — that gap is marked as unconfirmed rather than filled in.

What Almanac will not do. It does not invent a fact about a funder. An unpublished deadline is reported as unpublished, not replaced with a plausible-looking date, because a made-up deadline is worse than a missing one: a missing deadline gets checked, and an invented one gets relied on. It also keeps a hard line between a funder that has actually given to this organization before and a funder that merely looks like a good fit. A candidate is never dressed up as a confirmed prior funder.

What it cannot tell you. Form 990 filings reach the public a year or more after the fact, so a funder's priorities may already have moved. A foundation that funds this kind of work is not therefore open to you: many accept no unsolicited proposals at all, and some of the best matches here will be closed doors for reasons no filing records. The ranking is a judgment about fit, not a prediction of success. Treat every row as a lead to verify against the funder's own page before you spend real time on it.
