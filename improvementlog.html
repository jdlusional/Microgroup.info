Improvement Log · Enterprise

Each enterprise product (Almanac, Scribe, Concordance, Herald) improves continuously, and this page is the record from the user's side: what changed, when, and what it means for the organizations using it. Entries are curated. Internal engineering work that a user would never notice is left out, so what remains is the product getting better. The Public Data family (GIFT, Interlock, F4F) keeps its own log on jonathanlindavis.com.
