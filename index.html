Core Subjects & Expertise

Five disciplines, one measured approach

MICRO is an acronym: Management, Integration, Culture, Research, and Operations.

Management

Strategic direction and leadership capacity for organizations in transition or growth.

  • Strategic planning and organizational strategy formulation
  • Executive leadership development
  • Interim director services during leadership transitions
  • Interim or fractional campaign management for candidates, campaign committees, and political organizations

Integration

Programs, teams, and software brought into one working system, with training for the people who use it.

  • Software implementation
  • Programmatic and organizational integration
  • Organization-wide training on new systems and processes
  • Voter file, donor, and constituent database management

Culture

Team building and employee engagement aimed at collaboration that outlasts the workshop.

  • Organizational culture development
  • Team building and collaboration initiatives
  • Employee engagement strategies

Research

Applied research that grounds client decisions in evidence, drawn from the founder's background in legislative and policy analysis.

  • Employee pay analysis and market analyses
  • Policy impact studies and legislative or bill analysis
  • Opposition research
  • Electoral and campaign data analysis, including targeting and turnout modeling
  • Spatial and district-level analysis

Operations

Operational management focused on getting more out of the resources you already have.

  • Operational management and process improvement
  • Resource allocation optimization
  • Productivity and institutional effectiveness initiatives
