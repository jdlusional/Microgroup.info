Core Subjects & Expertise

Five disciplines, one measured approach

  • Executive leadership development

  • Interim or fractional campaign management for candidates, campaign committees, and political organizations

Integration

Programs, teams, and software brought into one working system, with training for the people who use it.

  • Software implementation

  • Organization-wide training on new systems and processes

Team building and employee engagement aimed at collaboration that outlasts the workshop.

Software Products

Purpose-built software for nonprofit grant-seeking

Almanac and Scribe are live and priced today; the rest of the line is built to varying degrees behind them.

Almanac

$1,500 / year

Finds and ranks funders and open grants that fit your organization, with a public source shown for every match.

Scribe

$1,500-$3,000 / year

Drafts a near-submission grant application from an Almanac match, fact-checked against your organization's own public information.

  • Standard: $1,500 / year, one draft per quarter
  • Pro: $3,000 / year, one draft per month

Concordance

Identifies the people who govern a funding foundation's board and maps introduction paths.

Built and deployed for one client · not yet in the standard catalog

Herald

Monitors funder pages on a client's watch list for cycle openings, deadline changes, and guideline updates.

In development

Atlas

Benchmarks a foundation's giving against aggregate state-level 990-PF data.

In development

Dossier

Reconciles a foundation's claimed giving against its own filed 990-PF.

In development
