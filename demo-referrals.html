Referral program

Refer an organization to Almanac or Scribe. If they sign a first paid subscription, you earn 5% of their first-year product price as a one-time bonus.

The 5% applies to Almanac and Scribe list price only. It excludes the Infrastructure & Updates add-on, and it is paid once, on the first year of a new contract, not on renewals. Full terms are below.

5%

of the referred organization's first-year Almanac and/or Scribe price, paid once as a referral bonus. See Terms for exactly how the base is calculated and when it's paid.

Connected to the pricing request form

How it works

The referral is recorded on the same form the referred organization uses to request pricing, so there's one record, not two separate systems to reconcile.

  1. Tell an organization about Almanac or Scribe

    Anyone can refer, at any time, an organization that isn't already talking with Jonathan Lin Davis about these products.

  2. The organization submits a pricing request and names you

    At demo-pricing, the optional "Referred by" section of the request form captures the referrer's full name, email, phone, and the date the referral was made, kept separate from the date the organization actually submits the form.

  3. The organization signs a first-year contract

    If and when that happens, the referral is confirmed against the terms below.

  4. You're paid the referral bonus

    5% of that organization's first-year Almanac/Scribe price, once the payment and timing conditions in the Terms section are met.

Read before referring anyone

Terms comprehensive

These terms govern every referral recorded through the pricing request form. They apply as written unless changed here.

  1. Who can refer. Anyone may refer an organization. Referring yourself, an organization you already work for or govern, or an organization already in active conversation with Jonathan Lin Davis about these products, does not qualify.
  2. What makes a referral qualify. The referral must be recorded in the "Referred by" section of the pricing request form, at or before the referred organization's first submission, including at minimum the referrer's full name and the date the referral was actually made.
  3. The referral base. 5% of the referred organization's first-year Almanac and/or Scribe list price, as shown on the pricing page at the time they sign. The Infrastructure & Updates add-on is excluded from this calculation entirely.
  4. One-time only. The bonus is paid once, on the organization's first annual contract. It is not paid again on renewal years, even if the organization continues as a subscriber.
  5. Timing. The bonus is paid after the referred organization's first payment has cleared and a 30-day cancellation or refund window has passed without the contract being cancelled or refunded.
  6. Payout method. Paid by check or ACH/bank transfer, at the referrer's choice, to the contact details provided at the time of referral.
  7. Tax reporting. If cumulative referral payments to a single referrer exceed $600 in a calendar year, a completed W-9 is required before payout, and a 1099-NEC will be issued for that year, per IRS reporting requirements.
  8. No guarantee. A referral bonus is contingent on a valid, signed first-year contract and cleared payment from the referred organization. It is not owed for a referral that does not convert to a paying contract. Any dispute over whether a referral qualifies is resolved at Jonathan Lin Davis's sole discretion.
  9. Program changes. This program, including the 5% rate, the base it applies to, and these terms, may be modified or discontinued at any time. A referral that already qualified under the terms in effect when it was made is honored under those terms.
  10. Not an agency relationship. Referring an organization does not make the referrer an employee, contractor, or agent of Jonathan Lin Davis, and a referrer has no authority to negotiate pricing or make representations on his behalf.

How this program works, and what it cannot tell you

Where this comes from. This is the standing referral program Jonathan Lin Davis offers on Almanac and Scribe, tied directly to the same rate card shown on the pricing page.

What this program will not do. It will not pay a bonus for a referral that isn't recorded on the pricing request form, and it will not pay more than once per organization's first contract.

What it cannot tell you. Whether a specific referral qualifies depends on the facts of that case against the Terms above; this page states the rules, it doesn't pre-approve any individual referral.
