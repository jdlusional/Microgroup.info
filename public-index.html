Services
ServicesOpen ↗
MICRO Group works across five named disciplines. Management means strategic planning and organizational strategy, including interim director service when a seat needs filling. Integration brings new software and workflows into an organization and trains the staff who have to use them. Culture work is team building and employee engagement aimed at collaboration that outlasts a single workshop. Research includes pay analysis, market studies, and policy impact work. Operations is about getting more out of the resources an organization already has. Any engagement beyond the two named products below is priced individually.
Products
AlmanacOpen ↗
A funder and grant match for a nonprofit, built from public IRS filings: it finds the funders and open grants that fit an organization and ranks them. $1,500 per year.
ScribeOpen ↗
A grant application drafter built from an Almanac match. Standard includes one draft per quarter for $1,500 per year. Pro includes one draft per month for $3,000 per year.
Infrastructure & UpdatesOpen ↗
An add-on to an Almanac or Scribe subscription covering ongoing technical support and system updates. $1,500 per year.
Who We Serve
Who We ServeOpen ↗
MICRO Group serves nonprofit, government, and political and legislative organizations. Nonprofit clients range from national organizations to local ones, across a range of missions. Government clients include municipal, state, and federal agencies and public departments. Political and legislative clients include campaigns, candidates, committees, and advocacy organizations, drawing on the founder's legislative-analyst background; this service line becomes active upon his departure from nonpartisan public-sector analyst work, effective July 31, 2026. Current operating counties are Austin-Travis County, San Antonio-Bexar County, Dallas-Fort Worth Metro, and Houston-Harris County. Requests and solicitations from beyond that footprint are welcome worldwide.
