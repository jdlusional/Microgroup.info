Products
AlmanacOpen ↗
A funder and grant match for a nonprofit, built from public IRS filings: it finds the funders and open grants that fit an organization and ranks them. $1,500 per year.
ScribeOpen ↗
A grant application drafter built from an Almanac match. Standard includes one draft per quarter for $1,500 per year. Pro includes one draft per month for $3,000 per year.
ConcordanceOpen ↗
Board-to-funder affinity mapping: finds the named individuals who govern private foundations whose grantmaking already matches a nonprofit's cause area, and maps who on the client's board could make the introduction. Deployed for one client; in development.
HeraldOpen ↗
A grant-cycle monitor watching the funder pages an Almanac match already surfaced: daily fetch, diff, and classify against cycle openings, deadlines, and guideline changes. In development.
AtlasOpen ↗
Benchmarks a corporate or private foundation's giving against real aggregate statistics computed from IRS Form 990-PF filings. In development.
DossierOpen ↗
Reconciles a foundation's claimed giving against its own filed 990-PF numbers and cross-references voluntary donation lists against public filings. In development.
Infrastructure & UpdatesOpen ↗
An add-on to an Almanac or Scribe subscription covering ongoing technical support and system updates. $1,500 per year.
