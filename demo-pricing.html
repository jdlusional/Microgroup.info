Pricing

Standing annual rates for Almanac and Scribe, and an optional plan that keeps every product an organization has purchased on current infrastructure. This is the same rate card every organization sees; nothing here is negotiated in the abstract before a request comes in.

Current prices are guaranteed through July 31, 2026: any request submitted and confirmed by that date locks in the rates shown here, regardless of any later change to this page.

Unlike the product demo pages, this page describes Jonathan Lin Davis's actual current pricing, not a simulation against a fictional organization. Submitting the form below does not create a contract; it opens a conversation, and final terms are confirmed in writing.

Products & pricing

Each product is priced and purchased on its own. There is no bundle discount: buying more than one product is the sum of what's listed below.

Almanac Standard

$1,500 / year
Matches refreshed quarterly (4 / year)

Ranked funder and open-grant matches for one organization, every match sourced to the funder's own public filing or page.

Funder & grant match · Pro

Almanac Pro

$3,000 / year

How this page works, and what it cannot tell you

Where these numbers come from. This is the standing rate card Jonathan Lin Davis quotes to every organization: the same Almanac, Scribe, and infrastructure prices shown here, not a number produced for a specific negotiation.

What this page will not do. It does not process payment and it does not create a signed agreement. Submitting the request form starts a conversation; it commits nothing on either side.

What it cannot tell you. Final terms, start dates, and any organization-specific detail are confirmed in writing after a request is reviewed. If a referral is involved, its terms are governed by the referral program, not by anything stated here.
